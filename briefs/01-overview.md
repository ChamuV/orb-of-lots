# Overview

## Assignment Summary

In this assignment, you will help the explorer and Professor of Finance, Jeremy Hunt, claim the Orb of Lots hidden within the Temple of Gloom.

The task involves navigating an underground cavern, locating the Orb, and escaping before the cavern entrance collapses.

Additional rewards can be obtained by collecting gold during the escape phase.

---

## Assignment Phases

The assignment consists of two principal phases:

1. Exploration Phase
2. Escape Phase

---

## Exploration Phase

During the exploration phase:

- The cavern layout is unknown.
- Only local information is available.
- The objective is to locate the Orb of Lots.
- Fewer movement steps result in a higher score.

Implementation is completed in:

```java
student.Explorer.explore()
```

---

## Escape Phase

During the escape phase:

- The cavern layout changes.
- The full map becomes visible.
- A time limit is imposed.
- Gold can be collected to increase the final score.
- The objective is to escape before the cavern collapses.

Implementation is completed in:

```java
student.Explorer.escape()
```

---

## Individual Coursework Scope

For the individual version of the coursework:

### Included

- Exploration Phase

### Excluded

- Escape Phase

The escape phase remains useful as documentation and as a possible extension project but is not assessed in the individual coursework.

---

## Primary Goal

For the individual coursework, the primary objective is:

```text
Develop a correct and efficient exploration algorithm that reliably finds the Orb of Lots.
```

---

## Learning Objectives

The assignment focuses on:

- Graph traversal
- Search algorithms
- Software engineering practices
- Testing
- Documentation
- Version control
- Algorithm optimisation
